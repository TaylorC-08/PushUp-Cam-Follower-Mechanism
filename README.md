Design constraints:
○ Design a cam and follower.
○ Cam must provide smooth linear motion.
○ The cam motion must be perpendicular to the motors rotation. (If the motor axis of rotation is the x-axis, the cam is rotating about the y-axis)
○ The cam must move something up and down and the object has to be articulated.
○ Example: A person jumping, where the legs are bending at the knees and hips.

Gravity may be used.
